## The Three-Part Structure (from Turner's Introduction, PDF p. 527)

Turner identifies three subtractates:
1. **Voice of the First Thought** (NHC 35,32-36,27; 40,29-41,1) — Protennoia descends as light to give shape to fallen members
2. **Speech of the Thought** (NHC 42,4-27; 45,2-12; 45,21-46,3) — Protennoia descends to empower with spirit/breath
3. **Word / Logos of the Thought** (NHC 46,5-6; 47,5-22; 49,15-22; 50,9-12) — Protennoia descends in human likeness, introduces the Five Seals, restores members to the light

**Key for schema encoding:** The three descents are SOTERIOLOGICAL (rescue paths), NOT part of the cosmological structure. The primary schema encodes only the cosmological descent. The triadic nature of Protennoia is encoded as a node attribute, not a branching structure.

## Key Passages for Schema Encoding

### The Invisible Spirit and Protennoia/Barbelo (level 0-1)
> "I, the Thought of the Father, Protennoia, that is, Barbelo, the [perfect] Glory and the [immeasurable] Invisible One who is hidden. I am the Image of the Invisible Spirit and it is through me that the All took shape, and (I am) the Mother (as well as) the Light which she appointed as Virgin, she who is called Meirothea, the incomprehensible Womb, the unrestrainable and immeasurable Voice." (PDF p. 531 / NHC XIII 35,1-16)

**Key:** Protennoia explicitly identifies AS Barbelo. She is "the Image of the Invisible Spirit" — meaning the Invisible Spirit is above her, she is his first thought-image.

### Autogenes/Christ (level 2)
> "Then the Perfect Son revealed himself to his Aeons who originated through him, and he revealed them and glorified them and gave them thrones... They blessed the Perfect Son, the Christ, the only-begotten God." (PDF p. 531 / NHC XIII 35,18-25)

### Four Luminaries (level 3)
> "The first Aeon, he established [over the first]: Armedon, Nousanios, Armozel; the second he established [over the second Aeon]: Phaionios, Ainios, Oroiael; the third over the third Aeon: Mellephaneus, Loios, Daveithai; the fourth over the fourth: Mousanios, Amethes, Eleleth." (PDF p. 531 / NHC XIII 35,35-36,5)

### The Origin of Yaltabaoth from Eleleth (level 3-5 transition)
> "Then there came forth a word from the great Light Eleleth and said, 'I am King! Who belongs to Chaos and who belongs to the underworld?' And at that instant his Light appeared radiant, endowed with the Epinoia. The Powers of the Powers did not entreat him and likewise immediately there appeared the great Demon who rules over the lowest part of the underworld and Chaos. He has neither form nor perfection, but on the contrary possesses the form of the glory of those begotten in the darkness. Now he is called 'Saklas,' that is, 'Samael,' 'Yaltabaoth,' he who had taken power; who had snatched it away from the innocent one (Sophia); who had earlier overpowered her who is the Light's Epinoia (Sophia) who had descended, her from whom he (Yaltabaoth) had come forth originally." (PDF p. 531-532 / NHC XIII 36,15-32)

**Key:** In Trimorphic Protennoia, Yaltabaoth arises when Eleleth speaks — different mechanism than Apocryphon (where Sophia's direct fall produces Yaltabaoth). Sophia/Epinoia pre-exists Yaltabaoth and was "snatched" rather than giving birth to him.

### Sophia/Epinoia's Response (level 4-5 transition)
> "Now when the Epinoia of the [Light] realized [that he (Yaltabaoth)] had begged him (the Light), for another [order, even though he was lower] than she, she said, 'Grant [me another order so that] you may become for me [a dwelling place lest I dwell] in disorder [forever.'] And the order of the entire house of glory [was agreed] upon her word." (PDF p. 532 / NHC XIII 36,32-40)

### Yaltabaoth Creates (level 5-6-7)
> "And the great Demon began to produce aeons in the likeness of the real Aeons, except that he produced them out of his own power." (PDF p. 532 / NHC XIII 37,4-7)

> "...the Archigenetor of ignorance reigned over Chaos and the underworld and produced a man in my likeness." (PDF p. 532 / NHC XIII 37,22-25)

### Protennoia's First Descent (soteriological — NOT in primary schema)
> "I have come down and reached down to Chaos. And I was [with] my own who were in that place. [I am hidden] within them, empowering [them and] giving them shape." (PDF p. 532 / NHC XIII 37,30-35)

## Structural Differences from Apocryphon of John

| Feature | Apocryphon | Trimorphic Protennoia |
|---|---|---|
| Barbelo/Protennoia | Barbelo = separate from higher powers | Protennoia = Barbelo (explicitly unified) |
| Sophia's fall | Sophia acts without consent → gives birth to Yaltabaoth | Eleleth speaks → Yaltabaoth appears → snatches power from Sophia |
| Adamas | Separate node (Perfect Man, level 3) | Not a distinct structural level |
| Protennoia's nature | Not present | Triadic: Voice / Speech / Word |
| Salvific focus | Descent of Pronoia (appendix only) | The text's entire structural framework |
